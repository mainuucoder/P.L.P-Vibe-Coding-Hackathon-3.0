Shiriki Services

Forums

Our Forums module provides a space where citizens can share their thoughts and opinions on various issues affecting their communities. Users can join existing discussions or start their own threads. Our moderators ensure that the discussions are respectful and constructive. The forum topics are carefully chosen to cover a wide range of topics, including community development, education, healthcare, infrastructure, environmental conservation, and social welfare, among others.
We believe that engaging in constructive dialogue is key to finding solutions to complex problems. Our forums are designed to encourage people to share their opinions and ideas, and we welcome all voices, regardless of their political affiliations, social status, or gender.

Surveys

We allow subscibed organizations to regularly conduct surveys to gather feedback on various topics. Your input is valuable to different stakeholders, and it can be used to improve services and policies. By participating in the surveys, you can help shape the future of the community. The surveys cover a wide range of topics, including public services, infrastructure, social welfare, environmental conservation, and community development, among others.
We use various methods to ensure that our surveys reach as many people as possible, including online surveys, telephone surveys, and face-to-face interviews. We also ensure that our survey results are easily accessible to the public, so that everyone can see the feedback we receive and how we use it to improve our services and policies.

Bursary and Scholarship Applications:
Our platform provides opportunities to organizations offering bursaries access to a fully automated bursaries management processes. It makes it easy for students to apply for bursaries and scholarships. We believe in investing in our youth, and we want to support them in achieving their academic goals. You can find information about available bursaries and scholarships on our platform and submit your application online.
Stay Informed

Our platform provides the latest news and updates on government initiatives, policies, projects and key publications. You can access this information from our news feed and stay informed about what's happening in our community. We also provide links to other relevant sources of information, including government reports, policy documents, and research papers. We believe that informed citizens are critical to good governance. Our platform is designed to provide citizens with accurate and timely information about government initiatives, policies, and projects. We also encourage citizens to engage with us on our platform and ask questions, provide feedback, and share their views on various issues.
We also train and sensitize members of the public on different government initiatives
